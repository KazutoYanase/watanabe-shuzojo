The origin of the Mannen brand name:

Mannen means 10,000 years in Japanese. There is an old Japanese proverb: Cranes live for 1,000 years, turtles live for 10,000 years. The name was chosen to share and celebrate the reputed long lives of cranes and turtles.

Seasonal productsUnfiltered Asahi-Mannen

This is the first brand that Koichiro Watanabe (4th generation) worked on when he took over the family business in 2002. It is a shochu that has reproduced a traditional flavor that can only be achieved by ladling out minimum oil content. Its characteristics are intense sweetness and aroma. It uses rice koji from Natsu no Emi, a new variety of rice produced in Miyazaki Prefecture.

Raw ingredients sweet potatoes (Kogane Sengan variety produced in Miyazaki Prefecture) / rice koji (black koji): rice produced in Miyazaki Prefecture
Alcohol % 28%
Period of sale From January until sold out from stores
Bottle Size 1800ml
